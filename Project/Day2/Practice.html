Title: Buddha Dhatu Jadi

Buddha Dhatu Jadi which is known as the Golden Temple of Bandarban is situated at Pool Para in Balaghat I Bandarban. It is also known as Mahasukh Mandir. Although it is named as Golden Temple, there is no statue made of gold in the temple. It is now known as the Golden Temple for its golden color.

Title: Nilachal

Nilachal, often referred to as the "Darjeeling of Bangladesh," is a picturesque hilltop destination in Bandarban. Known for its breathtaking views and serene atmosphere, Nilachal offers a perfect retreat for those looking to escape the city's hustle and bustle..
